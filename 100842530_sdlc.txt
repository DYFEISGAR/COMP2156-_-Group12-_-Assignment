What is the importance of learning DevOps engineering and what is its role in a software deveopment cycle?

DevOps is key in the software development cycle as it bridges the gap between development and operations, making sure of a smoother and faster delivery of applications and services to the end-users. By incorperating DevOps practices, engineers can significantly improve teamwork, automate workflows, and affiliate continuous integration and continuous deployment (CI/CD), which leads to increased efficiency, reliability, and faster time to market for software artifacts
